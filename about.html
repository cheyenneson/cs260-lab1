Jess Harlow is a portrait photographer and filmmaker based in London. Her work is characterised by positivity, colour, character and a graphic sensibility.

Touching on these inspirations, Jess embraces playfulness in her work and believes portrait photography should be uplifting and inclusive - There are enough terrible things in the world for us to see. If you look at diverse facial imagery, it should be good to look at, and make you feel good.

Jess applies her singular aesthetic to fashion, beauty and portrait assignments for titles such as British Vogue, Modern Weekly China, Time Magazine, Twin Magazine & W Magazine. She has also lent her energy and optimism to a diverse range of commercial campaigns.
